If you want to upload your own reads, you need to have two separate .fasta files: reads from the forward strand and from the reverse strand. In this case, we can apply paired-ended alignment algorithm to your data.

The best place to get your sample reads is 1000 Genomes website. This website contains sample reads of 1000 individuals of different ethnic origins.

The best place to get your sample reads is Mouse Genomes Project. This website contains sample reads of 16 breeds of mice.

We strongly advise you not to take datasets with the data on the whole genome but with low coverage. It's much better to run a more targeted research (on a single chromosome, or even on a particular set of genes, but to have a decent coverage of at least 20-30x).
