Order a custom project

Launch a fully customized pipeline via our Web-portal

Description

We can migrate the functionality of any of your processing pipelines to our infrastructure. You can use any software and datasets you like, and you do not need to configure anything in your browser yourself to make this transition - we will do the job for you.

The process works the following way: you send us either your script or simply a description of what your pipeline does and which software/utilities you use for this. We will make it run on our infrastructure and optimize it for parallel processing. As a result, you will have private access to your processing pipeline via a convenient Web-interface, plus our cluster-based facilities to run it efficiently.
