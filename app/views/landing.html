TECHNOLOGY

Use preconfigured cluster facilities out-of-box

Bioinformatics is in many ways all about processing a lot of data, which often can't be done on a client machine. For example, the size of a human genome file is 3.5 Gb, and when you try to index it on your MacBook Air with 4 Gb RAM, it takes hours and hours.

We empower our users with an amazing opportunity to execute their pipelines on a full-blown cluster without knowing anything about distributed computing. Every pipeline is launched with a single click in an Apache Mesos virtual container with 32 cores and 16 Gb RAM, so processing takes minutes rather than hours.
