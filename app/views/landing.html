Get your results quickly

We value your time, so by "quickly" we literally mean getting your output in 2 clicks in your browser. We will allow you to focus on the interesting part of analyzing your results via a user-friendly interface of any application convenient for you. No more debugging of shell scripts and wading through manuals. No need to configure a lengthy pipeline in a web application with buggy and crooked interface.

Use cluster facilities

Bioinformatics is a data intensive area. With access to our cluster facilities you can process gigabyte files on the fly without bothering about IT infrastructure. We run Apache Mesos and DC/OS on an HPC-cluster to execute your computations efficiently.

Store your data

Up to 2 GB of free storage available for your files at any time for free. Manage your input and output files in a convenient way.

Advanced options

If you are a professional user who wants to run his own pipeline, with specific tuned parameters, we can address your needs. Let us know, and we'll create a custom pipeline in our system that will be available for you in your user account. On our side, we will take care of efficiently running it in parallelized environment of an HPC, so you won't need to bother about writing these algorithms on your own and will be able to focus on your area of expertise.
