TECHNOLOGY

Use preconfigured cluster facilities out-of-box

Bioinformatics is all about processing gigabytes of raw data, which usually can't be done efficiently on a client device. For instance, a basic task such as indexing a human genome and aligning the reads to it takes hours and hours on a typical Macbook Air.

We run your pipelines in an HPC-cluster.

We empower you with an ability to do any operation in less than a couple of minutes. We use enterprise-class parallel computing technology stack to make it work (DC/OS, Apache Mesos, OpenStack). Each of your pipelines can be launched in a container with up to 60 vCPUs and 128 Gb RAM. For heavylifters, we also offer MPI and InfiniBand at request.

Speed up your data processing, and let us take care of the IT infrastructure for you.

STORAGE

Store all your datasets in a private cloud

We provide up to 5 GB free storage for your input files. This doesn't include the bulky genome data files, which we keep stored in our shared repository and available for you at any time.

We also have a library of sample datasets with dozens of interesting cases.

You can run the pipeline jobs on these sample datasets to see how the process works, and of course the output from these jobs will not count towards your storage space. Every dataset can be downloaded for free and later used in any bioinformatics software you want.

FLEXIBILITY

Get instant access to your legacy pipelines in your browser

We can also address your needs if you are a professional bioinformatician. Say, you have your own custom and tuned set of pipelines, but your hardware is insufficient to run them fast enough. Or you don't want to dig into details of how to run your pipelines in a cluster environment.

We can implement your legacy pipelines in our system for you.

Send us an email with either your script or even simply a description of the steps you take when processing you data. We will implement your pipeline in our system making it accessible for you in a single click via your private account.
